Bio

Daniella "Saffron" Youkhana is a digital artist born and raised in California, who is presently calling Portland, Oregon home. She is currently attending Arizona State University to obtain a BA in Digital Culture, and previously attended the University of California, Santa Cruz where she studied Video Game Design. At ASU and in her free time, she intends to study a wide variety of topics and focus her degree towards: 2D and 3D game design, 3D animation, digital illustration, character design, and digital accessibility. At present, her creative interests lie in fantasy character art and digital painting, and with Japanese anime being one of her first and biggest (and still current) inspirations, she intends to try her hand at producing fan merchandise.
Outside of art, Daniella works as a barista at Starbucks and is actively part of the union organizing committe at her store. Gaming takes up a large portion of her free time, whether it be casual playing a video game, or developing lore in her various TTRPG campaigns. Fiction and fantasy are a huge driving force for Daniella; it is safe to assume that at any given time, one could fine her immersed in fiction.
