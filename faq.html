Frequently Asked Questions

Not really, but I want to share anyway!

When did you start drawing?

Some of the earliest (and fondest) memories I have of drawing are from middle school, when I would stay up too late watching anime on the weekends with my best friend, exchanging doodles with each other. I know I've loved art since a young age; I spent all of my time in school drawing, including all over my notes and homework. I drew in MS Paint with my mouse before I was gifted my first tablet. I was glued to my computer back then, drawing and drawing and sharing it with friends. As I grew older and life came with more responsibilities, as mroe and more people told me I wasn't good enough for an art career, the time I spent drawing was nearly zero. It wasn't until recently that I started drawing consistently again. When did I start drawing? When I was little, of course, but when was I reminded that it's never too late to pick something back up and try again, especially if you love it? Now. I'm 28 years old and I'm excited to be growing again and working my way towards an art career.

What else are you interested in?

So many things! The ADHD has made me try it all, but I've come to realize that as long as whatever I'm doing involves creativity, then I enjoy doing it. At work, I try to find some extra time to make fun, new drink recipes and practice latte art. At school, I've been focusing on web design and going down the list of Adobe Creative Suite programs. At home, you can find me cooking, gardening, playing games, watching shows, or spending time with my friends and pets.

What software do you use?

I primarily use Clip Studio Paint to draw. I'm using a lot of Photoshop for school, but I'm not ready to move over to PS for drawing yet. Too many brushes I'd have to replace... and the 3D feature is so nice... Maybe one day!

What hardware do you use?

I use the HUION Inspiroy Dial Q620M. I draw on a 27" monitor. My computer is a custom build so it goes vrrrooooooom!
